What is DermAI?

DermAI is an AI-powered skin lesion analysis tool designed to help you decide whether a skin lesion warrants a dermatologist consultation. It works with photos taken directly with your smartphone camera or uploaded from your gallery.

How does it work?

01

Upload — Take or upload a close-up, focused photo of the skin lesion. The app automatically checks image quality and relevance before proceeding.

02

AI Classification — A Vision Transformer model trained on the ISIC 2024 dataset classifies the lesion as Benign or Malignant, along with a confidence score.

03

Explanation — Gemini Flash explains the result in plain language, applying the ABCDE rule (asymmetry, border, color, diameter, evolution).

04

Follow-up — Ask follow-up questions about the lesion and receive detailed, conversational answers from the AI.

Features

  • Benign / malignant classification with confidence score
  • Automatic image quality and relevance validation
  • ABCDE-rule based observations in plain language
  • Conversational follow-up on lesion-related questions
  • Session history saved and accessible anytime
⚠ Important disclaimer
DermAI is an informational screening tool only — it does not replace professional medical examination or diagnosis. Never make a medical decision based solely on this application's output. If you have any concerns about a skin lesion, consult a dermatologist.
